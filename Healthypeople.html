About Us

What

Frankly, it was hard to even know what the website was about. My team of 3 did a complete UX/UI overhaul on HealthyPeople.gov so that future visitors might have a more streamlined and intuitive experience.

Context

Why

For this Case Study, my team completed an unsolicited redesign for a government website that was frustrating to use. We chose Healthypeople.gov because of its unorganized content structure and horrendous user flow.

Key Takeaways

Conslusion

If I learned anything throughout this process, it's that the process is what matters. Having a structured timeline to accomplish all of the little pieces makes putting the puzzle together much easier come time to develop. Always take the time to do thorough user research, and think through why we make each and every decision is critical to a well-thought-out design.
