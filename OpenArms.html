Case Study: Open Arms of MN

UI/UX Web Redesign

Simply put, openarmsmn.org was broken. This non-profit website was severely lacking a thoughtful user flow, which made signing up to volunteer challenging (which is the last thing a non-profit wants), and signing up for their benefits was harder still! My team and I went in and designed from scratch new flows for the multiple users, and turned a broken process into one that keeps volunteers coming back to Open Arms of MN.
